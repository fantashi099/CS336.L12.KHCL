di chai haimai thi maimi chan

nai wan ni thi chan dai phop thoe

thoe thi hen nai wan ni

at mi bangthi namta yang rin lai
mai penrai diao man ko hai
thoe mai tong tham mai tong saichai
chan ru wa chan yang wai
maichai khwam phit khong thoe khae chan eng thi yang

nai khwam fan yu thuk khuen

hai rukhit thueng sak phiang nai
chai yang mi phiang khae thoe
